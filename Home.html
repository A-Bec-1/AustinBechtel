Helldivers 2, a game developed by Arrowhead Game Studios, and published by Sony on February 8, 2024 is a cooperative third person shooter set in the satirical setting of Super Earth. A direct sequel to Helldivers, Helldivers 2 combines elements from third person tactical shooters and horde shooters against to different hostile fronts, the Terminds and the Automatons.

This website is a personal fan project that lists and describes my favorite weapons, armor, loadouts, and other aspects of the game. It also directs you to useful websites that give detailed information about the game and its lore.
