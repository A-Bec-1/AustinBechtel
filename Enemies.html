Super Earth's Enemies

Currently, there are two major enemy factions that threatens Super Earth's control over the Milky Way Galaxy, the insect horde of the Terminds, and the cold machine menace of the Automatons. Both war fronts are entirely different, each requiring different loadouts and strategies.

The Terminids

Once docile creatures, the Terminids have become agressive and territorial, constructing massive hives across planets, and attacking anything relating to humanity. The terminids, while dangerous, are vital to Super Earth, since their dead bodies decompose into Element-710, which is necessary to power fast than light travel for all Super Earth aircraft and spacecraft.

The Automatons

The Automatons are robotic machines hellbent on expanding and tearing control away from Super Earth.
